Access GoogleColab
We will use GoogleColab for the workshop, to access GoogleColab please follow these instructions:
Go to https://colab.research.google.com
Click Sign in (top right)
Log in using your Google account
If you don’t have one, click 'Create account' and follow the prompts
Accept any prompts asking for permissions
Once signed in, click 'New Notebook'
✅ You’re ready to use GoogleColab
